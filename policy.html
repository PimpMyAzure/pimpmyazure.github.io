§2. The basis for the processing of personal data
1. Personal data are processed by the Administrator in accordance with the law, in particular with the Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council (EU) 2016/679 of 27 April 2016 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data and repealing Directive 95/46 / EC (hereinafter referred to as "GDPR") in order to:

a.to conclude and perform the contract concluded on the terms specified in the Website's regulations (pursuant to Article 6 (1) (b) of the GDPR);

b. answering Internet users' questions related to the Website and its functioning (pursuant to Article 6 (1) (f) of the GDPR);

c. using the newsletter service, including sending commercial information, on the basis of expressed consent (Article 6 (1) (a) of the GDPR);

d. fulfill legally binding obligations on the Data Administrator, pursuant to art. 6 sec. 1 lit. c GDPR (e.g. accounting and tax obligations);

e. pursuing or securing claims (pursuant to Article 6 (1) (f) of the GDPR).

2. Providing personal data is voluntary, however, the consequence of failure to provide data may be, depending on the case, the inability to use the services provided by the Website, the inability to obtain answers to the questions asked or the inability to receive the newsletter.

3. The User should not provide the Administrator with personal data of third parties. However, if he provides such data, he always declares that he has the appropriate consent of third parties to transfer the data to the Administrator.

§3. The scope of personal data processing
1. The Administrator processes the scope of data provided by the user in the content of the issue addressed to the Administrator.

2. Data provided by users are used only for: performance of the contract concluded on the terms specified in the Website's regulations, answering questions, sending a newsletter, including commercial information about the Administrator and its products and services, and for statistical purposes.

3. The administrator uses IP addresses collected during internet connections for technical purposes related to server administration. In addition, IP addresses are used to collect general, statistical demographic information (e.g. about the region from which the connection is made).

§4. Control of personal data processing
1. The User is obliged to provide complete, current and true data.

2.Every user whose personal data is processed by the Administrator has the right to access their data and the right to rectify, delete, limit processing, the right to transfer data, the right to object to data processing based on the Administrator's legitimate interest, the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing (if the processing is based on consent), which was made on the basis of consent before its withdrawal.

3. The use of the rights specified in the paragraph above may be implemented by sending an appropriate request to the address kontakt@kursk8s.pl , providing the user's name and e-mail address.

4. The user has the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory body if he considers that the processing of his personal data violates the provisions of the GDPR.

Personal data may be transferred to entities processing them at the request of the Administrator, i.e. marketing agencies, partners providing technical services (developing and maintaining IT systems and websites). Personal data will not be transferred to a third country / international organization.

§6. Storage period and other information regarding data processing
1. Personal data will be kept only for the period necessary to achieve the specific purpose for which they were sent or for the purposes of complying with the law.

2. If the user has consented to the use of personal data in connection with the use of the newsletter service, personal data will be processed until the consent is withdrawn.

3. Personal data will not be processed in an automated manner by the Administrator.

§ 7. Cookies
1. The website collects information contained in Cookies.

2. The website uses the following types of Cookies:

a. Advertising - enabling the delivery of advertising content tailored to the User's interests;

b.analytical - collect information on the manner of using the Website, the type of website from which the User was redirected to the Website, and the number of visits and the duration of the User's visit to the Website;

c. functional - they allow to adjust the content of the Website's website to the User's preferences and to optimize the use of this website; in particular, these files allow to recognize the User's device and properly display the content of the Website.

3. The website uses Cookies of the following nature:

a. Permanent - they are stored in the device after closing the browser (end of the session) and expire after one year;

b.session - they remain stored on the device after closing the browser (ending the session) and are used to save information from a given session.

4. The User may object to the use of Cookies by changing the web browser settings.

5. Restrictions on the use of Cookies may affect some of the Website's functionalities, and even prevent the use of the Website.
